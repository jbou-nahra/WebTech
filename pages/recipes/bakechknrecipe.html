Ingredients

  • 6 Cloves of Garlic
  • 1/2 cup Olive Oil
  • 4 Quarter Chicken Pieces
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Italian Seasoning
  • Chicken Broth
  • Aluminum Foil
  • Vinegar or 1 lime
  • 1/2 cup Olive Oil

(Optional)

  • Mushrooms
  • Carrots
  • Potattoes
  • Rice
  • Pasta
  • Onion
  • Bell Pepper

Baked Chicken - pieces

  1. Preparation. We will start off by making Tum, an arabic garlic souce. Crush or puree garlic in mashing bowl or a food processor, slowly adding olive oil. Add a tea spoon of salt and a tea spoon of lime. Do this process very slowly such that the mixture doesn't break, until oil is done. Set aside. Clean chicken and soak in water with lime or vinegar.
  2. Season chicken with Salt, Pepper and Italian Seasoning such that the chicken is adequately covered. Also, cover chicken with Tum. Can let sit for about 3 hours for best marinade. Can also be cooked immediately if needed.
  3. Sear in frying pan for a few minutes until golden.
  4. Transfer to baking dish and cover with aluminum foil, add a can of chicken broth and bake at 350 degrees for 40 min.
  5. Remove foil and cook until golden brown. You can add mushrooms, potatoes to the baking dish if desired.
  6. Serve with rice.
