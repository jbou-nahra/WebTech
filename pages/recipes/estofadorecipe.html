Ingredients

  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1/2 cup Olive Oil
  • 4 Quarter Chicken Pieces
  • 1 Onion
  • Garlic Powder
  • 1 Pack Mushrooms
  • Black Olives
  • 1 Can Tomatoe Paste
  • 1 Can Crushed Mushrooms
  • Basil
  • Oregano
  • Thyme

Chicken Estofado

  1. Preparation. Finely chop onion, tomatoe, green pepper and black olives. Cut mushrooms into desired size.
  2. Season chicken with salt, pepper and garlic such that the chicken is adequately covered. Can let sit for about 3 hours for best marinade. Can also be cooked immediately if needed.
  3. Sear in frying pan with a little oil for a few minutes until golden.
  4. Add all other chopped ingredients except tomatoe paste and crushed tomatoes.
  5. Once the veggies are transparent and soft, add the tomatoe paste and crushed tomatoes.
  6. Place in oven and bake at 350 degrees for 40 min.
  7. Serve with rice or pasta.
