Ingredients

  • 1 large onion
  • 1 medium green bell pepper
  • 3 ripe tomatoes
  • 5 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 cup capers
  • 1 medium bottle manzanilla olives with pimento
  • 1 carton chicken broth
  • 3 Chicken breast
  • 2 Chicken legs and thigh
  • 2 cups sliced mushrooms
  • Salt
  • 2 bay leaf
  • Basil
  • 1/2 cup Parsley
  • small can crushed tomatoes
  • Complete season
  • Saffron
  • Garlic powder

Rice

  • 2 cup rice
  • Butter 1 tbsp
  • 1 cup chopped cilantro
  • 1 tbsp powder chicken bouillon

Chicken Estofado

  1. Preparation. Season chicken with complete seasoning, basil, and garlic powder. Let set for 2 hours.
  2. Sear in oil of choice until brown and set aside.
  3. Sautee vegetables.
  4. Add crushed tomatoes.
  5. Add chicken broth.
  6. Add bay leaf and other seasonings and let bubble.
  7. Add chicken , chopped parsley and cover.
  8. Simmer on low for 1 hour or until thighs are soft.
Rice.
  1. Wash rice.
  2. Put to boil 3 cups water.
  3. Add butter , bouillon.
  4. Add rice and cover on low.
  5. Let cook without opening for 15-20 minutes.
  6. Open and add cilantro .
  7. Fluff with a fork.
