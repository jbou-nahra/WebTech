Bake Chicken

One of the first dishes we had to put on our menu. The classic baked chicken, a common staple. We that since this was such a basic staple we should give ahead and give it an early try. We asked mom for recipe and she gave us a quick explanation over whatsapp and we followed those. Pictures of whatsapp messages below. Also, there is a link to the proper recipe page here:

Bake Chicken Recipe

First issue we ran into was a dull knife to cut the chicken. We needed to add a new knife and knife sharpening tools to our toolset. Next we needed a second cutting board, so that we could work together. The tum also took a while as I had to use a manual grinder, a food processor is something we would like to get down the line. I think it came out well and it didn't break which was good. The olive oil made the color a little darker than the vegetable oil would have it but it is much healthier.

We browned the chicken and put to bake, pouring in the chicken broth but I think we put too much or we should have waited till after we baked. This is something we would need to clarify with mom as we think it was a little too watery at the end. Overall, it came out delicious though and we enjoyed it very much.
