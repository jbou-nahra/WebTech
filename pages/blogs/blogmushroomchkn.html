Mushroom Chicken

We simply found this recipe while browsing Youtube. It looked so delicious and seemed simple enough. Luckily, we we were correct on both points. This is probably one of the first dishes I can say I was personally very proud of as it came very close to not having any issues. This was definately a confidence booster. I did feel however that I didn't move fast enough when it came to moving from one step to the next as was suggested. However, the end result was still very good.
