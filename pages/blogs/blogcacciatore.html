Chicken Cacciatore

The valentine special. We chose this dish to do for valentines day. We knew we wanted something tomatoe based with olives. We loved the aroma of this dish and we feel it matched the occassion. It was relatively simple and we believe it came out really well over all. The leftovers were even better the second time around.
